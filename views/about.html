About Me:

I am a biology major who turned to web technologies after starting a career in Technical recruiting. My passion for tech and the growing industry helped me confirm my decision to turn to a new career.

At the end of 2016, I joined General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program where I started my path into what I am now, a fullstack developer. I love creating web applications where I specialize in the MEAN stack but definitely open to learning more about other software engineering technologies.

In my free time, I tinker with learning about UX designs and best practices and expanding my skills in programming; Right now, I'm doing so by trying to understand React and due to Grow with Google's Android Basics Scholarship, android development. At the same time, I am trying to fill in the computer science skills that would help me stengthen my algorithms and data structure knowledge through code challenges as well as trying to learn from Khan Academy.

Besides that, I'm a big foodie and a gym enthusiast. There's always something about finding a great restaurant and eating great food and documenting that experience via Instagram or Snapchat. There's always something new to try.
